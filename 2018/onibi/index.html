Kouga: “Over here, dumbass!”

Subaru: “Gami-san, how come you’re in the parking space!? Teleportation!?”

Kouga: “Are you stupid? I just walked here while taking your call.”

Subaru: “Gami-saaan! Thanks so much!”

Kouga: "Whoa! Don’t jump at me!

I swear I’ve told you over and over to stop being touchy with me!"

Subaru: “I know, I know. But really, thank you☆”

Kouga: “Keh! Anyway, you shouldn’t even hide it in the first place, you sly bastard. Just say it like it is: that you’re scared of being alone in the dark.”

Subaru: "Umm, but my fear felt a little different from how you guys are scared of ghosts or older sisters.

When I’m alone in a dark place――

It feels like I’m the only person in the world, and I just get increasingly anxious.

A dark world where I’m all alone isn’t sparkling at all… I don’t like it…"

Kouga: “Are you stupid?”

Subaru: “Gami-san, you call me ‘stupid, stupid’ too much! You bully!”

Kouga: “I call you stupid because you’re stupid. Where are we? The school’s parking space, right? Can’t you see the ‘sparkling’ bikes around you?”

Subaru: "…

Pfft…! Ahahahaha! You’re right! The wheels and the reflectors are all so shiny☆"

Kouga: "This isn’t some dark world, ya know.

Me, Adonis, Anzu, and Forehead, we were just a short distance away. And you’re saying you’re alone? What the hell?

We’re all here because you wanted to look for the fireball, right?"

Subaru: "Yeah. That’s… That’s right, huh?

I wasn’t alone at all!"

Kouga: “Tch. Fine if you understand. So what do we do? Look for the others?”

Subaru: "Ah, wait! My phone’s ringing… it’s Sally!

Hello, Sally? Are you okay? Yeah. Yup. So Anzu and Occhan are also with you? Ahh, that’s great♪

I have Gami-san with me, too. Okay, got it! Then we’ll go over there to regroup! Sure. Bye-bye~☆"

Kouga: “What did Forehead say?”

Subaru: “Sally said they’re all gathered in the garden terrace. We should go back there too!”

> Garden Terrace

Mao: “Ooi, Subaru! Kouga! Over here♪”

Subaru: “Sally!”

Mao: “Guh!? Don’t crash into me like a monkey! You almost knocked me over!”

Adonis: “I’m glad you two are safe.”

Kouga: “You guys look fine too. Well, not that I care.”

Mao: "Subaru, sorry for not answering your call.

I dropped my phone in the garden terrace, y’see~ Man, that was such screw-up. I only noticed your call when I got it back."

Subaru: "So that’s why you didn’t pick up, Sally… Anzu seems to have her phone turned off too.

Ahaha, Anzu’s taking out her phone in a fluster.

Out of battery? Ah, so that’s why.

No, you don’t need to apologize. You couldn’t have done anything about it.

Anyway, what was with you two back there, Sally, Gami-san?

Just as some loud clanging-rattling sound came from the kitchen, we heard you two scream…

And saw you ran out of the kitchen in a hurry? I was really surprised."

Mao: “Ah~… Well, about that――”

Subaru: "Aaah! A fireball! Sally, guys, look! There’s a fireball in the garden terrace interior!

…Huh? But it’s too… small for a fireball? Maybe?"

Kouga: “Nn? (sniff, sniff) This smell is…”

Jin: "Ohh. Akehoshi, Oogami, so you’ve safely returned.

You brats gotta cut it out, seriously~

Don’t play around in the school when it’s night. If someone finds you, I’m the one who’ll get scolded. Mainly by Kunugi-sensei."

Subaru: “Eh, why is Sagami-chan here?”

Kouga: “So you’re the source of the smoke smell! It stinks like hell!”

Jin: "Ah, sorry sorry. I forgot you’re sensitive to smell, Oogami. Wait a sec ― I keep a pocket ashtray, see.

Look, I’ve put it out. Doesn’t smell as bad anymore, right?"

Subaru: "So the fireball was…

The light of Sagami-chan’s smoke? No way…"

Mao: "Yep. To give a rundown of what happened in the garden terrace――

Kouga and I were looking for the fireball in the kitchen, with little results…

Until suddenly, we saw a silhouette coming in from the backdoor.

That frightened the life out of me that I knocked over the cooking utensils left on the counter, you see~

The clattering freaked me out even further that I screamed. And Kouga got caught up in it."

Kouga: “Tch, it’s cuz you just screamed outta nowhere. Of course I couldn’t help screaming too, dammit.”

Mao: “So in the panic, we just scampered out of the kitchen with everything we had.”

Jin: "And the silhouette coming in from the backdoor was me.

I got curious since I heard voices in the school when the lights should be out already~

And when I came to take a look, I found you guys in the garden terrace."

Kouga: “Damn. If I was a lil’ bit calmer, I could’ve tell with the smell. You really played me, Forehead.”

Mao: “I wasn’t trying to play you! But I’ll admit my panic got us into this mess. My bad.”

Adonis: "Either way, I am glad nothing happened to the two of you, Oogami, Isara.

Anzu is nodding with me. Nn? Ah, so you also feel bad for getting everyone involved by saying you wanted to see the ghost, Anzu."

Subaru: “I’m the one who first said I wanted to catch the fireball. I’m sorry, guys.”

Adonis: “You two have nothing to worry about. We’re all safe. And I thought it was fun, searching for the school’s seven mysteries with friends at night.”

Mao: “Looking back on it, yup. And it really did end up being something more ordinary than we thought, after we looked☆”

Jin: “The ‘Seven Mysteries of Yumenosaki Academy’, huh…? I’m probably at fault for most of those…”

Subaru: “Eeeh, it was you all along, Sagami-chan!? The portrait of Bach growing a long hair too!?”

Jin: "That one was~ because I accidentally sprinkled chalk dust on the painting, probably.

When I took it to the back of the school in secret so I could beat the dust out, a student happened to see me.

Seemed like the chalk dust made it look like his hair grew out. It became a story of how I exorcised the painting’s ghost behind the school building. Ahaha."

Subaru: “Eh~? Do people really make that kind of mistake?”

Jin: “Don’t ask me. It’s not my fault they came up with that story.”

Subaru: “Then what about the Anzu ghost sighted in the garden terrace?”

Jin: “What’s that? Anzu, you were a ghost?”

Mao: “Ah no, the capta… Morisawa-senpai said he ran into an Anzu ghost in the garden terrace.”

Jin: "Aah, I remember him saying something like that. About seeing Anzu there?

But I didn’t see her so I can’t say~? You weren’t in the kitchen that time, right Anzu?

Well, that’s just how it is. You should know after seeing how Isara was like: When people get into a state of panic, they lose the calm to make a sound judgment~

Morisawa was really scared, so wasn’t he just seeing things?"

Subaru: “The piano playing on its own and the female student at the rooftop were also you, Sagami-chan?”

Jin: “Ahh~ That’s not me… but someone I know. Don’t worry; they’re human.”

Subaru: “Bummer… I feel silly after knowing the truth.”

Mao: “I’m relieved~♪”

Kouga: “Keh! So I was right; ghosts don’t exist!”

Adonis: “It’s a little unfortunate. If they exist, I was hoping to see them.”

Jin: "Anyway. You’re satisfied, right?

I’ll take the spare key and return it, so out you go. Dismissed, dismissed~"

Mao: “Let’s go back home, guys. Good night, Sagami-sensei.”

Subaru: “Sagami-chan, bye-bye~☆”

Jin: “See ya~ Take care on the way home, you kids~”

(I only started smoking in the garden terrace right before Akehoshi and Oogami came, but…

I guess I better keep the secret to myself.

Can’t be fanning their curiosity carelessly, or they might disrupt my peaceful night rest like today♪)
