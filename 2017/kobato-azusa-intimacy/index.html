Fufufu, I think you’ll be surprised to find out!☆ About ‘The Love Letter Great Strategy’!

That’s a good look of surprise, Onii-chan☆

Hwe…? Ah! I said love letter, but I don’t mean it’s a confession of my romantic love, okay? I mean the general philanthropic kind of love?☆

Azusa will send you as deep a love as what she showed to everyone in the Tennis Club and Health Committee…☆

But yes; perhaps, if it’s Azusa and you, Onii-chan, a different type of love could bloom… Fufufu☆”

It’s fine, though, since Azusa is in the middle of cleaning; she can’t worry about how her hair looks. But Azusa wishes she could stay the prettiest version of herself in front of you, Onii-chan.

Ah, yes. There was no injured person to take care of today, and the tennis club’s practice is over. So Azusa is cleaning the office.

Yesterday… Saturday was Kurumi-chan’s turn to be on stand-by… She’s a little clumsy, that girl. She kind of broke stuffs all over and left the place dirty~☆

She’s always that way. So if the day before was Kurumi-chan’s shift, Azusa makes sure to clean up the place like this. Azusa loves to clean and turn the place all sparkling~☆

(heart thumping) …Umm, so it doesn’t seem like you’re injured anywhere.

Uh-huh, so you felt a little dizzy, and thought it better to take a rest in the Nurse Office just in case?

It must be from exhaustion. I think. You’re always busily running all over, Onii-chan.

Uh-huh… So you were helping out with Basketball Club’s preparation today, since they had a practice match?

Oh, Onii-chan. You work too much. Let Azusa spoil you for the good boy you’ve been~☆

Mm-hmm, you skipped breakfast today because you woke up too early? And your lunch got eaten by Komatsu-senpai from the Basketball Club?

You have to take your meals properly, or you’ll find yourself passing out from anemia. Since you worked so hard today on an empty stomach, it’s no wonder you felt dizzy from exhaustion.
