✿ MINI | Nagamachi Yae

Posted on 2019-12-07 Edited on 2019-12-13 In engr , Transfer Student (Otouto) , Nagamachi Yae , Angie (Anzu)
Symbols count in article: 1.3k Reading time ≈ 5 mins

Accessible from the “Stories by Student > Nagamachi Yae > Other” tab in Ensemble Girls! Memories.

Yae’s substory, set on her birthday. It doesn’t actually have a title, so it’s hard to document…

Before the second arc of Engirls’s main story, this was the only source of the “Angie can drive” tidbit.

Nagamachi Yae

Written by: Akira

Yae: “Onii-san, good evening!

Hey hey, I swear it’s just for tonight, so can you let me stay over? I’m not listening to your answer! I’m coming in!

Mugu!? Don’t grab me by my head, you’re gonna break my neck!

I’ll tell you the details later… Wait, whoa? Who’s this? It’s a woman I don’t know!

Are you Onii-san’s girlfriend?

Ah, you’re Onii-san’s big sis?

Come to think of it, I did hear about Onii-san having a big sis! Hello!

You’re Onii-san’s Onee-san… So you’re ‘Onii-onee-san’… or ‘Oninee-san’ for short?

Like Demon Big Sis, ahaha!

Mugyu!? Why are you hugging me!? You like how I call you ‘Oninee-san’!?

You’re so weird. I think you and Onii-san might be alike.

Oninee-san, what do you mean ‘Can we keep this girl?’…? Mggaah, I’m not a pet you can just pick up, okay?

I’m Nagamachi Yae; Kimisaki Academy 1-B! Nice to meet you. I’ll be in your care for today~

I’m not gonna cause any trouble, so relax, okay?

If you guys would just lend me your backyard, I’ll roll a sleeping bag there and take care of myself.

I have provisions too! Onigiri, donuts, milk~

So anyway, that’s how it is! I’ll be in your care! The backyard is this way, right?

Yaaay, are you going to carry my sleeping bag and other stuff for me, Oninee-san? You’re so nice!

You’re really like Onii-san’s Onee-san!

Mugugu! Why do you keep grabbing my head… Okay, I know I’m short so my head is in an easy place to grab…

‘Explain about this properly’? I guess I should… Umm, see~

I’m running away from home for a bit!

Mom and Dad were so blind it pissed me off, so I ran from home!

I thought about staying at Ichika’s, but on my way there, I spotted your house…

So I came to stay over at your place!

I spent the night here during the stay-over party too, so I thought you’d let me if I ask.

My parents will quickly know when I hide at Ichika’s, so there’s not much point in running away in the first place.

But my parents don’t know Onii-san’s house yet, so I don’t have to fear being caught, you know? It’s perfect!

So I’ll be staying here! I’ll help with the chores too! Just leave it all to me!

Eh, my reason for running away? Umm, well, it’s nothing really big.

Uhyaa!? Oninee-san, why are you lifting my skirt? Pervert!

No, no, no, I’m not ‘being abused’ or anything! Sure, my thighs are full of fresh scars…

But they’re all from my Softball Club practices, or from taking care of the animals at the school barn, or adventuring through the underground tunnels…

I just have a lot of chance to get scars in my routines, that’s all!

Ugyaa!? Why are you both hugging me!? ‘You can think of our place as your home, Nagamachi-san’…?

Don’t turn this into some cheap soap opera!

What’s with these siblings!? You’re both such a pain!

No no, I think you’re still misunderstanding me! It’s not like I’m not getting along with my family, we’re always such cozy bunch!

Don’t sympathize with me! I’m not a pitiful girl!

Huh wait, what are you doing, Onii-san? You’re calling my home?

W-Why do you know my home number? You’re so full of mysteries!

Stop that! I need to teach Dad and Mom a lesson!

So you see, I used to be a really selfish brat… I always went around saying ‘I want this’ and ‘I want that’ and troubled my parents a lot for it.

I never did chores and only ever complained too. Guess it was my rebellious phase or something…

But I reflected on many things after entering high school…

So I tried to act the serious, good girl, but then my parents just took it to the extreme, you know?

Even though it’s my birthday, they didn’t give me anything. I wondered why, so I asked them

And they answered, ‘You didn’t say anything so we thought you didn’t want anything’!

Isn’t that ridiculous? That really ticked me off! I’m so so angry right now!

So I ran away. I didn’t do anything wrong, right?

Then let me stay. I won’t be going home for a while.

Eh, what? The phone? Didn’t you listen to what I said, Onii-san?

Wait, actually, don’t tell me everything I said got heard to the other end of the line…?

Eeh, you’re telling me to take the phone? H-Hello?

What, it’s you, Onii-chan! What’s up? What about Dad and Mom?

They got worried for me and are searching everywhere right now…? Hmph, it’s too late to act like proper parents!

I’m furious, okay? You can play the ‘good kid’ in that house if you want, Onii-chan!

…Y-Yeah. Well, I see. So they were trying to celebrate my birthday?

Uwaah, don’t eat all the cake! Leave my share too!

I know, I used to be really selfish… I would go really crazy when they didn’t do as I said…

I guess it’s not so strange they ended up thinking I didn’t want anything, since I didn’t say a thing to them.

Got it. I’ll speak with Dad and Mom.

I should’ve talked it out with them since the beginning. I was in the wrong, too.

If Dad and Mom are looking all over for me, then it’s perfect.

Tell them to drop by somewhere on the way and buy me a present, will you?

I want a hamster! Yeah, I want a pet!

You know, I’ve been really friendly with animals living in the underground tunnels and the school barn, lately!

I’ve been thinking about raising a pet at home, too.

I’ll leave it to you, Nii-chan! Bye bye! I’ll be home soon.

Ihihi~…

Ah, yeah! I’ve made up with them! Or rather, my brother’s gonna eat all the cake if I don’t go home now!

I’m going back home now! I’m sorry to bother you two!

Uhya!? Onii-san, why are you carrying me on your shoulders? You’re going to take me home?

Oninee-san will go get the car and drive me home, so you’ll be carrying me until the parking lot? Wow, so she has a driving license?

Aww, what a thorough service! I’m happy!

If I can’t make up with my parents in the end and run away again, at that time I wanna become a child of this family!”
