[Intimacy] Konan Yako, Chapter 1

Written by: Akira

> Rooftop

Yako: “Zz… Zz…

Nn, ngh… Let me sleep for a little longer. Lately it doesn’t matter how much I slept, I just couldn’t shake off my sleepiness. Gosh, I’m not supposed to be that old yet…

…Mggha!?

Wha—hey, don’t pinch my nose! Don’t shake me! I get it, I’m waking up!

Geez. Couldn’t you wake me up a little more gently…? What’s up, seriously…?

You’ve got some guts to mess with me. Who is it — Chizuru? Kuro? Angie…?

Hmm? Huh, who the heck…?

Sorry, do you see a pair of glasses anywhere around there? Real round ones… I can’t see anything without my glasses, okay?

Aah, thanks. That’s the glasses. Pass that to me already, quick!

All right, got my sight restored!

…Sorry, seems I was half-asleep. Dreamed about the past and stuff. I shouldn’t have slept on the rooftop, huh? Yawn…

Hmm? I looked prettier without my glasses, you say? We just met and you have the cheek to flirt with me, brat? You’re my junior, aren’t you?

…Okay, before that, who the heck are you, seriously?

Sorry. I think I’m seriously still half-asleep. I don’t really grasp the situation… I stayed up late yesterday preparing for our next stage, so I’ve been skipping classes to sleep the whole day.

Yeah, I’m part of the Drama Club. I take care of the production and all of those behind-the-scene stuffs myself; so doesn’t matter if the sun’s up or down, there’s always a pile of work to dive in.

…Say, haven’t we met before?

Hmm, no? You sure? I feel differently, though…

Anyway, that there’s a boy in our school means—you’re the rumored “Transfer student-kun”, aren’t you?

I see, so you’re the guy…

Say, you got any interest in acting? Our Drama Club is lacking actors, see. It’s a given but we’re especially short on male roles, and it‘s not like we can ask the teachers either, right?

Hmm, what? You’re giving me coffee?

Thanks… This will be a good eye-opener. Gulp~

You’re quite the thoughtful one, Transfer student. Can’t you help our club out, even with just the small tasks? I mean, seriously.

Oh, right. Do you have some business with me?

Hmm. So Chizuru was looking for me? She said I might be on the rooftop, so you’ve come to check? I see, thanks for your hard work.

Wait, it’s this hour already! Damn, I overslept! Thanks a bunch for waking me up, I’ve got work waiting for me so I’m going on ahead!

I’ll return the favor if I remember! Let me repay you for the coffee, at least.

Okay… Catch you later, then, Transfer student~”
